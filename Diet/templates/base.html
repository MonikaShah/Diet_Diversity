Overview

We endeavor to focus on innovative strategies for creating awareness using online Nutrition Education Programs (e-NEP) and door-step delivery of vitamin and mineral rich leafy vegetables from community based nutri-garden/microgreens to overcome the urgent need of micronutrients to combat malnutrition and anaemia among ICDS beneficiaries by using a WEBGIS platform and involvement of school children.

School

We aim to create a platform for peer-learning where the school students become the nutri-leaders within their households. Trained Nutri-educators and Mentors will ensure and monitor the participation of schools and students. Using this platform, school students will be enrolled in an interactive e-Nutrition Education Programs (e-NEP) to improve their own health and nutritional status and bring a change in their household eating behavior.

Nutri Graden(Poshan Vatika)

Nutri-Leaders from the school will be educated in these concepts of nutri-gardening, microgreens cultivation, nutrition, health and hygiene. To put their education in practice, the school will provide the land for development of micronutrient rich nutria-garden/microgreens. These nutri-leaders will, in turn, share the produce with selected Anganwadis from the community who in turn will distribute its precious cargo to ICDS beneficiaries (children with SAM, MAM and anemic women and adolescent girls) to tackle the micronutrient deficiency within the vulnerable population. This will not only help the ICDS beneficiaries but develop a set of life-skills among the school students.

ICDS

The ICDS services focus on providing adequate nutrition to reduce malnutrition among the vulnerable population within the community. These include SAM/MAM children, pregnant women, lactating mothers and adolescent girls. The ICDS cadre (Mukhya sevikas and Anganwadi workers) strive to achieve this goal. With the help of this platform, we aim to train the beneficiaries in the concepts of nutrition to bring about a behavior change. The beneficiaries will have direct and quick access where their queries and feedback will reach the ICDS cadre and monitoring their health and nutritional status is easier.
