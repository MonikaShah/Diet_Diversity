Overview

The central aim of this project is to focus on innovative strategies for creating awareness using online Nutrition Education Programs (e-NEP) and door-step delivery of vitamin and mineral rich leafy vegetables from community based nutri-garden/ microgreens to overcome the urgent need of micronutrients to combat malnutrition and anaemia among ICDS beneficiaries by using a WEBGIS platform and involvement of school children.

School

Schools would be approached to identify and educate school-going children. Trained Nutri-educators and Mentors will ensure and monitor the participation of schools. These trainings will be conducted using e-Nutrition Education Programs (e-NEP) for the mukhya sevikas, anganwadi workers and school children.

Nutri Graden(Poshan Vatika)

This is a multi-approach pilot project which involves innovative strategies will create Nutri-Monitors. These children would be appointed as Nutri-Leaders (10-16 years of age) who have been educated in these concepts of nutri-gardening, microgreens cultivation, nutrition, health and hygiene. These nutri-leaders will, in turn, share the produce with selected Anganwadis teachers who in turn will distribute its precious cargo to selected ICDS beneficiaries (children with SAM, MAM and anemic women and adolescent girls).

ICDS

The ICDS cadre (Mukhya sevikas and Anganwadi workers) will be trained in the concepts of nutrition mentioned above. The beneficiaries will have direct and quick access where their queries and feedback will reach the anganwadi workers and mentors.
